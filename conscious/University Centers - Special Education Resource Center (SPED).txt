Special Education Resource Center

The SPED Resource Center and Therapy Room is located at the Northeast Wing of the New Elementary Laboratory School Building of the South La Union Campus. It serves as a classroom for the students in special education. Moreover, the SPED Resource Center is a diagnostic center for the assessment of mathematical, scientific and language skills of children. The room houses shelves of reference books and specialized materials used for SPED and Guidance Counseling. It has several tables, racks, play area, and dividers. It is air-conditioned for the comfort of users and clients.

The room adjacent to the resource room is a therapy room where assessment and SPED interventions are being held.
