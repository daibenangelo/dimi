ADMISSION PROCESS - FOREIGN STUDENTS ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS

Must meet the following:
DMMMSU College Admission Test
Certificate of Secondary Curriculum Completion
Original Transcript of Record
Personal Data
Affidavit of Support
Alien Certificate of Registration (ACR)
TOEFL/IELTS result (for non-native English speakers)
Student Visa
Security Clearance from Embassy
Resident Guarantor of Character
CHED Permit
Alien Fee

Admission depends on course slot availability.
