The SLUC Dancesport League, established in 2009, focuses on training students in dancesport, a competitive ballroom dancing sport emphasizing physical prowess, coordination, teamwork, and musical interpretation. Its goals include enhancing members' skills, fostering harmony within the community, training future dance professionals, promoting discipline, and providing an outlet for dance enthusiasts. The league has hosted seminar-workshops and competed in various tournaments, including its annual Free Seminar-Workshop.
