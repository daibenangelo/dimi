Welcome to the DMMMSU Library!

The library supports critical thinking, collaboration, communication, and creativity through its resources and services. It aids instruction, research, and community programs by providing well-organized materials to support the university’s development goals.

Key objectives include:
Offering well-selected resources for DMMMSU’s academic programs.
Meeting research and reference needs of students, faculty, and staff.
Providing lending services for various users.
Facilitating open-shelf access with user guidance.
Enabling electronic information access via the internet and multimedia tools.
Building networks with other libraries for broader access and staff training.

Ask DORLA, the DMMMSU Online Reference Library Assistant, for help! Designed for the “new normal,” DORLA answers queries and supports users remotely.
