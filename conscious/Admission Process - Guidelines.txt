Enrollment Procedure for New Students:
Program Chairperson: Fill out the Enrollment Permit and Registration Form.
Registrar: Submit requirements and have student data encoded.
Medical & Dental Unit: Submit forms for Enrollment Permit approval.
SAS Unit: Apply for Free Higher Education (FHE) form approval.
Program Chairperson: Enlist subjects and assess fees.
Cashier: Pay fees not covered by FHE.
Registrar: Validate enrollment.

Enrollment Procedure for Returning Students:\
SAS Unit: Apply for FHE form approval.
Program Chairperson: Enlist subjects and assess fees.
Cashier: Pay fees not covered by FHE.
Registrar: Validate enrollment.
