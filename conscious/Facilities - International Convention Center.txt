The 66.5M International Convention Center at DMMMSU's North La Union Campus, inaugurated on February 8, 2021, has a first floor capacity of 500 and a second floor capacity of 315. It is located in Sapilang, Bacnotan, La Union, with an area of 1,963 sq.m. Two hostels, each worth 8.792M pesos, can house 52 individuals.
