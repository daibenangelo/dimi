LEAVE OF ABSENCE

1. Students may take a leave for up to two consecutive terms with approval.
2. An approved written request, signed by the parent/guardian and endorsed by the Department Chair/Director/Dean, must be submitted to the Registrar and approved by the Chancellor.
3. Approved leaves are not counted in the maximum period to complete a degree.
4. Students must return the semester immediately after their leave to maintain regular status.
5. Students must follow the revised curriculum unless granted approval to continue the old one.
6. After five years of absence, re-enrollment forfeits previously earned units.
