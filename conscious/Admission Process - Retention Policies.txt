ADMISSION PROCESS - RETENTION POLICIES

Sec. 1
25%-36% grades of 5.0: Next term load limited to 75% of normal; issued a warning.
37%-50% grades of 5.0: On probation; load limited to 50% of normal.
Above 50% grades of 5.0: Cannot re-enroll in the same course; may shift to another course.

Sec. 2
100% grades of 5.0: Permanently barred from re-admission.
