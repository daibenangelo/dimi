ADMISSION PROCESS - ADDING, CHANGING, AND DROPPING SUBJECTS

1. Grounds: Allowed within the prescribed period for:
Time conflicts
Missing prerequisites
Dissolved/canceled subjects
Deadline:
Undergraduate: Within 3 days from class start
Graduate: Within the first 2 sessions

2. Instruction Changes: Recommended by the adviser/tutor, approved by the DOUS Director. Individualized learners must finish within 6 months; delays result in incomplete grades, completable within 1 year.
3. Dropping Before Midterm: Allowed with approval (Teacher, Chairperson/Director/Dean, Registrar). Mark: "D".
4. Dropping After Midterm: Without valid reasons, a failing grade (5.0) is assigned.
