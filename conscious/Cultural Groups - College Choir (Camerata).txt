CAMERATA, the college choir of DMMMSU-SLUC, showcases the vocal talents of its members to promote Filipino heritage, goodwill, peace, and harmony. Membership is open to college students with good moral character and voice quality, subject to an audition. Eligible members with at least one semester of membership can apply for a scholarship covering tuition, renewed if academic requirements are met. Established in 2017, CAMERATA actively participates in and wins choral competitions and hosts singing seminars and performances locally and nationally.
