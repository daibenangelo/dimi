Admission Process - Evaluation

Sec. 1: Residence Credits
Academic units earned and validated by DMMMSU for a degree are residence credits.
Only residence credits are used to evaluate subjects and units for a degree.

Sec. 2: Student Classification
Full-time/Regular Student: Carries the full load as per the curriculum.
Part-time/Irregular Student: Carries less than the full load.
Transferee: Started studying elsewhere and is now registered in DMMMSU after qualifying for admission.
Foreign Student: Not a Philippine citizen.

Sec. 3: Curricular Level Placement
Placement is based on a matrix, excluding courses for validation when determining the year level.
