The University Infirmary provides medical and dental services for students and employees. It includes separate rooms for males and females, a treatment room, a records room, and a maternity clinic with a delivery room and baby crib nursery. The facility also has a ramp for accessibility.
The Infirmary Services offers routine medical and dental check-ups to students year-round, including during the pandemic, while following IATF protocols for in-person check-ups.
