The DMMMSU-SLUC Alumni Affairs Unit (AAU) held an Alumni Orientation for graduating students on May 30-31, 2022, led by Dr. Janice Rhea P. Cabacungan. The program aimed to strengthen ties between future alumni and the Alumni Affairs Office, raise awareness about its support services, and discuss potential social projects. Dr. Lher Verell Palabay, Director for Alumni Affairs, emphasized the importance of alumni connections, while Dr. Jesus Rafael B. Jarata, Director for Internationalization, urged students to give back to the community. Alumni guest speakers shared career advice and tips for securing employment.
